Welcome to My CS50W Final Project

A little about the project:

I tried to address a couple of real world problems I have when starting to think about this project. One of the issues I have had is that I can't keep track of my tasks everyday and it's quite demotivating to find out at the end of the day that I missed some of the things I was supposed to do. So I made a purely Javascript based TODO site where I can keep my tasks and cross off/delete the ones that I have completed. Secondly, I used Django to make a Recipe storing website for me where you can add and save your recipes with all the ingredients and an estimated cost which comes in handy. There's also a fun randomizer function that gives you random recipes for everyday, I call it 'meal of the day'
